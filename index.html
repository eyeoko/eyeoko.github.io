Bio

Jos De Roo graduated in 1979 as Master of Science (ir.) at KU Leuven
and did a doctoral study on image computing from 1979 till 1982.

He was co-founder of ICOS Vision Systems in 1982 and worked as
R&D engineer on computer vision applications until 1985.

From 1985 till 2021 he was working at Agfa HealthCare and Dedalus
as R&D engineer in the successive areas of digital medical hardcopy,
medical gateways, advanced clinical applications and clinical analytics.

Since 2009 he is developer of the EYE reasoner and since 2021 he
works part time as R&D engineer on Semantic Web reasoning at UGent.
