Jos De Roo

R&D Engineer

Bio

Jos De Roo graduated in 1979 as Master of Science (ir.) at KU Leuven and
did a doctoral study on image computing from 1979 till 1982.

Around 1980 he was building wind turbines and turbine 5 produced hydrogen.

He was co-founder of ICOS Vision Systems in 1982 and worked as R&D
engineer on computer vision applications until 1985.

From 1985 till 2021 he was working at Agfa and Dedalus as R&D engineer
in the successive areas of digital medical hardcopy, medical gateways,
advanced clinical applications and clinical analytics.

Since 2006 he is developer of the EYE reasoner.

Since 2021 he is working on Weye at IDLab of Ghent University.
